Lagos State @night

Lagos State is a state in South West, Nigeria. Of the 36 Nigerian states, Lagos is the second most populous state but the smallest in terms of land mass (Area: 3,577 km²). Lagos state is the country's (Nigeria) economic and cultural hub. It is known for its bustling city life, vibrant arts scene, and historical significance. Lagos is home to over 20 million people, making it one of the most populous cities in Africa. the state was founded in 27 May 1967 with it's capital in Ikeja.
